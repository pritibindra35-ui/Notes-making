Why Effective Note-Taking?

  • Understand complex concepts clearly.
  • Improve memory and recall.
  • Study efficiently for exams.
  • Develop critical thinking skills.
Well-structured notes can save hours during revision!

Popular Techniques

  • Cornell: Separate notes, cues, and summary.
  • Mind Maps: Visual diagrams to connect ideas.
  • Outline: Hierarchical bullet points.
  • Charting: Compare topics side by side.

Revision Strategies

  • Review notes within 24 hours.
  • Use color-coded summaries.
  • Teach the topic to someone else.
  • Practice retrieval regularly.
Spaced repetition boosts retention dramatically.
